## Reunions

### 2026 (30-year reunion)

Calling the Colts from the Class of 96, it's time to celebrate!!
It's been 30 years since we walked the halls, cheered at the games, performed in the auditorium, and posed for those iconic dance photos.
We've gotten a little older, a little wiser, and some gray hair, but we can still party like it's 1996!

Come join us at The Break for a casual night of reconnecting and memory making!
There will be yummy food, a fun DJ, and plenty of space for us to get a good ol' fashioned Stomp going!

Food is included, but beverages are on your own.
This is a 21+ venue, so ID will be required.

The price will go up the closer we get, so please sign up soon!
If there is a hardship, please reach out to one of the members of the Reunion Squad.
We can't wait to see you all again!

- **Date:** Saturday, 22 Aug 2026
- **Time:** 7:00pm
- **Location:** [The Break Sports Grill, 4760 South 900 East, Millcreek, UT 84116](https://maps.app.goo.gl/gYmK16KBVw61XLqm9)
