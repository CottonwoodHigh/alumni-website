## Reunions

### 2026 (50-year reunion)

You're invited to our Golden Jubilee!
Join us to celebrate, reconnect, and reminisce over a catered dinner.

- **Date:** Saturday, 15 Aug 2026
- **Time:** 6:00pm–9:00pm (catered buffet dinner 6:30pm–8:30pm)
- **Location:** [Cottonwood High School, 5715 South 1300 East](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Cottonwood+High+School%2C+5715+S+1300+E%2C+Murray%2C+UT)
- **Cost:** $45.00 per person
- **Payment:** Venmo @Juliann-Olsen, or mail a check to Robyn at 5831 W Perez Ave, Visalia, CA 93291 (write "50th reunion" on the check)

:::note[Please register!]
Register by Thursday, 30 Jul 2026.
RSVP on Facebook to Christine Bickley Hobbs.
:::

There is also a pre-event the night before: Friday, 14 Aug 2026 at 8:00pm at A Bar Named Sue, 3928 South Highland.
