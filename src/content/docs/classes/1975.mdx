## Reunions

### 2010 (35-year reunion)

The 35-year reunion was a full weekend: Friday night at the Hog Wallow, a picnic at noon Saturday at the Spruces in Big Cottonwood Canyon, and the main event Saturday evening at the school.

- **Date:** Friday and Saturday, 20-21 Aug 2010
- **Time:** 6:00pm Saturday (main event)
- **Location:** Cottonwood High School
