## Reunions

### 2025 (summer barbeque)

The class gathered for a summer barbeque in late August 2025.
Photos are in the [class website gallery](https://www.classcreator.com/Salt-Lake-City-Utah-Cottonwood-1973/class_gallery.cfm?gallery_link_id=15595).

### 2024 (50+1 reunion)

The class celebrated its 50-year milestone a year late with a "50+1" reunion in the summer of 2024.
Photos are in the [class website gallery](https://www.classcreator.com/Salt-Lake-City-Utah-Cottonwood-1973/class_gallery.cfm?gallery_link_id=15595).
