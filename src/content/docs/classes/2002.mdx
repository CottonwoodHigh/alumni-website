## Reunions

### 2022 (20-year reunion)

Our 20-year reunion was an evening of mingling with fellow Colts, a slideshow, and delicious food and drinks.
Photos from the event, attendee bios, and a digitized 2002 yearbook are available on the [official class website](https://chs02.wordpress.com/).

- **Date:** Saturday, 9 Jul 2022
- **Time:** 6:00pm–11:00pm
- **Location:** [Twenty & Creek, 2050 East Creek Road, Sandy, UT 84093](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Twenty+and+Creek%2C+2050+East+Creek+Road%2C+Sandy%2C+UT+84093)
- **Dress code:** Date night casual
- **Price:** $70 pre-sale, $85 at the door
