### 2018 (40-year reunion)

Our 40-year reunion brought the '78 Colts together at Little America in downtown Salt Lake City.

- **Date:** Saturday, 29 Sep 2018
- **Location:** Little America Hotel, 500 S Main Street, Salt Lake City, UT
