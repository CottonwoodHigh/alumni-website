## Reunions

### 2017 (10-Year Reunion)

- **Location:** Cottonwood High School
- **Date & Time:** Saturday, 5 Aug 2017, 7-9:30pm
- **Parking:** Plenty of parking in the lots.
- **Tickets:** $15/person if paid by July 21st, or $20 after July 21st
- **Special Notes:** You and a +1 are invited to attend

Our [10-year reunion](https://www.facebook.com/events/1885775445016611/?acontext=%7B%22event_action_history%22%3A[%7B%22surface%22%3A%22external%22%7D%2C%7B%22mechanism%22%3A%22attachment%22%2C%22surface%22%3A%22newsfeed%22%7D]%2C%22ref_notif_type%22%3Anull%7D) was held in the nostalgic inner courtyard at Cottonwood.
