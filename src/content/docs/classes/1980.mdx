## Reunions

### 2025 (45-year)

From [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/share/p/1bmPVrqkxa/):

> Well people, the party's over.
>
> Our 45 year class reunion was a smashing success!
> I was honored to work alongside Jevine Lane and Jesse Beales to help make it truly an amazing event.
> Big shout out to the volunteers and financial contributors who stepped up to help too.
> It would not have come together without your help.
> Everything might not have gone exactly as planned, but it was obvious everyone was having a REALLY great time catching up with other classmates.
> With over 170 people in attendance...for me anyway...there was just no way to catch up with everyone I wanted to.
> I'm already thinking of SO many classmates I saw from across the space, but never made it over to give a hug to, and say hello.
> It has really reminded me that, no matter how much time has passed, we all have a connection that will endure for the rest of our lives.
> Sending a big, sloppy, sweaty, (oy vay, it was hot), hug and a kiss to everyone who showed up.
> And to those of you who couldn't make it for one reason or another, you were definitely missed.
> It's time now to decompress...and get back to our regular lives.
> But keep in mind, we'll do this, (or something similar) again in five years.
>
> With much love,
>
> Barbara Morris (aka Babs)
