## Reunions

### 2025 (40-year reunion)

Our 40-year reunion, ["Alive and Kicking!"](https://www.facebook.com/share/p/1BSAB4fzJa/), was a dinner party on the deck of the Cottonwood Country Club, with a buffet dinner, cash bar, door prizes, a trivia game, photos, music, and plenty of time to reconnect.
Classmates were welcome to bring one guest.

- **Date:** Saturday, 13 Sep 2025
- **Time:** 6:30pm–9:30pm
- **Location:** [Cottonwood Country Club, 1780 East Lakewood Drive, Holladay, UT 84117](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Cottonwood+Country+Club%2C+1780+E+Lakewood+Dr%2C+Holladay%2C+UT+84117)
- **Attire:** Smart casual
- **Cost:** $85 per person or $170 per couple by 1 Sep 2025, $100 per person or $200 per couple after

### 2015 (30-year reunion)

Our 30-year reunion was held jointly with the classes of 1983–1987.

- **Date:** Friday, 7 Aug 2015
- **Location:** Cottonwood Country Club, Holladay, UT
