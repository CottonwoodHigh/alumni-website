### 2024 (30-year)

Colts '94!
Our 30 year reunion will be September 20-21, 2024.
**The main dinner event will be Saturday, September 21st!**

Let's collect together members of the class of 1994.
It's not just the cool kids, because let's be honest, nobody was that cool.
Let's try to get everybody together if we can.
Reach out to your friend groups and save the date!

- **Date:** Friday and Saturday, 20-21 Sep 2024
